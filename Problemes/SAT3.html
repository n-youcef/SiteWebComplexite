Problème NP-Complet

3-SAT
entrée: une formule propositionnelle \(\phi\) dont les clauses sont de taille exactement 3
question: est-ce que \(mod(\phi\))\(\neq ∅\) (satisfaisable)?

\(\phi\) = (\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\))\(\land\)(\(\lnot x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\))\(\land\)(\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_3\))
\(\phi\) = (\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\))\(\land\)\(\lnot ( x_1\)\(\lor\)\( x_2\)\(\lor\)\( x_3\))\(\land\)(\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_3\))
\(\phi\) = (\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\))\(\land\)(\(\lnot x_2\)\(\lor\)\( x_2\)\(\lor\)\(\ x_3\))\(\land\)(\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_3\))
\(\phi\) = (\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(x_3\))\(\land\)\(\lnot ( x_1\)\(\lor\)\( x_2\)\(\lor\)\( x_3\))\(\land\)(\(x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_3\))\(\land\)(\(\lnot x_1\)\(\lor\)\(x_2\)\(\lor\)\(\lnot x_3\))

\(\phi\) est elle satisfaisable ?
